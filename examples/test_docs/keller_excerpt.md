# Paleontological Analysis of Foraminifera from Deep Sea Cores

## Abstract

This study examines foraminiferal assemblages from Deep Sea Drilling Project sites in the Pacific Ocean. We document species distributions across the Eocene-Oligocene boundary.

## Methods

Samples were collected from DSDP Site 123 at depths ranging from 200-400 meters. Foraminifera were identified to species level using standard taxonomic references.

## Discussion

The planktic species dominate the assemblage, comprising approximately 60% of the total specimens. The benthic species are characteristic of middle bathyal depths.
